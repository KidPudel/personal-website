I spent substantial time designing a card system around chain ordering, card mutation, input tags, catalysts and inhibitors, and health, mana, and sanity combined into one resource. Losing cards was also intended to create exhaustion and connect strategy to an emotional sense of depletion.

The design remains unfinished. An earlier implementation became cluttered because logic and signals communicated in too many directions. That failure is part of the artifact: it sharpened my interest in explicit state ownership, clearer system boundaries, and data-driven structures.
