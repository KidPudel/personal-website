---
title: Discourses by Campfire
summary: A survival suspense game about a traveler stranded in a mountain forest.
prominence: featured
status: complete
engine: Godot
---

I built the winter forest, day and night environment, layered data-driven structure, 3D art, music, sound, and effects as one connected game experience. The project explores atmosphere, survival pressure, and the behavior created by interacting systems in an isolated landscape.

After a player reported that the Windows export was broken, I corrected the build immediately. That small incident is part of the project evidence too: a finished artifact includes the path by which another person can actually experience it.
