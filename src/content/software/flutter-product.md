I proposed replacing separate Android and iOS applications with one Flutter product, learned Flutter while building it, and carried the application from an incomplete need through production readiness.

The work included menu and category behavior, basket and ordering flows, payment and 3DS, maps and addresses, courier state, promotions, loyalty and bonuses, themes, device adaptation, performance, and repeated usability and reliability fixes. The strongest evidence is not framework specialization. It is the combination of product decisions, interaction behavior, and implementation required to make the complete application work.

The application remains anonymized. Selected diagrams or screenshots will only be added after their confidentiality and publication suitability are checked.
